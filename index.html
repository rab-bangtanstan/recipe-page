Strawberry Short Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

1. Step1:

Mix dry ingredients and cut in butter. Add egg and milk.

2. Step2:

Spread into a greased baking pan and bake until golden.reheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
