Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Step 1:

    Mix dry ingredients and cut in butter. Add egg and milk.

  2. Step 2:

    Spread into a greased baking pan and bake until golden.reheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans

  3. Step 3:

    Sweeten strawberries and let sit.

  4. Step 4:

    To make the icing, whip heavy cream with a dash of vanilla essence. Keep whipping until it forms soft peaks. Add soft pink food color to the icing if required

  5. Step 5:

    Place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread half of the strawberry mixture over the cake layer. Top with the second cake layer and spread remaining strawberry mixture over the top.

  6. Step 6:

    Your Strawberry Shortcake is ready!!. Serve and Enjoy!
